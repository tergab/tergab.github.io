About

Building things.
Understanding systems.

I've spent close to two decades working across the full spectrum of technology — from infrastructure and cloud platforms to software development and systems integration. That breadth gives me a perspective that's hard to get from staying in one lane.

These days I'm especially interested in Python tooling, mobile and desktop device internals, and the kind of low-level system knowledge that most developers never need — but that becomes fascinating once you start digging.

When I'm not at a keyboard, I'm usually somewhere on the trails outside Tromsø, watching F1, or convincing myself my next side project will be the last one.
